Instruction Manual
From-scratch usage guide and practical application
1. Rāga Finder: - Click swaras like Sa, Re, Ga, Ma, Pa, Da, Ni. - Then click Identify. - Result: You get the closest sample raga match. - Practical use: Quick melodic exploration and testing. 2. Rāga Creator: - Give a name if you want. - Select a rasa and choose swaras. - Click Generate. - Result: You get a created raga summary. - Practical use: Build your own concept raga. 3. 10,000+ Rāga Combination Option: - In the Rāga Creator tab, choose the combination mode. - Select a system and genre. - Click Generate Combinations. - Result: The app simulates large-scale possible raga-pattern generation and shows counts/sample outputs. - Practical use: Conceptual large-scale raga exploration. 4. Mudrās: - Type a mudra name, form, meaning, or beejakshara. - Result: Matching mudra entries appear instantly. - Practical use: Search mudra meaning and use in dance. 5. Sacred Search: - Select a sacred category from the dropdown. - Type a word or phrase such as Rudram, Namakam, Chamakam, Tat Tvam Asi, Lalita, Hreem. - Result: Matching sacred entries appear instantly. - Practical use: Quick sacred lookup instead of a huge visible library. 6. Metaphors: - Type symbolic words such as lotus, light, wave, heart, space. - Result: Matching metaphorical entries appear instantly. - Practical use: Philosophical and symbolic interpretation support. 7. Recording & Sound: - Click Play Tone to test sound. - Click Start Recording to record if microphone is allowed. - Stop, Play Latest, or Download. - Practical use: Voice and sound experimentation. 8. Print / Save / Share: - These act on the current visible section. - Practical use: Save or share what is currently on screen. 9. Language and Translation: - This beta is structured for multilingual expansion. - Full automatic translation across all content is for the enhanced version. 10. Beta Status: - This is a testing beta build. - Enhanced full-feature version is planned in the coming months.
